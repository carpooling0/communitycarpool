🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

I moved — do I need to resubmit?

Yes. If your home area or workplace has changed, your existing journey submission will be matching you to people near your old location. You should submit a new journey with your updated details.

Why resubmitting is necessary

Your journey submission stores the specific home area (From) and workplace (To) you entered when you registered. The matching algorithm uses these locations to find compatible commuters. If you've moved, these coordinates are now wrong — and you'll either get no matches or matches with people who aren't actually near you anymore.

There is currently no way to edit an existing journey submission. The correct approach is to submit a new journey.

How to resubmit

  1. Go to the Community Carpool homepage.
  2. Fill in the form with your new home area and the same (or updated) workplace.
  3. Use the same email address as before — the new journey will be linked to your existing account.
  4. Submit as normal.
ℹ️
Both journeys will be active — Your old submission stays in the system alongside the new one. You'll see both on your matches page. The old journey will expire naturally after 30 days of no new matches. If you'd prefer it removed immediately, contact us via the support page.

What about existing matches from before I moved?

Any matches found for your old journey — including any mutual matches — remain visible on your matches page. If you have an active mutual match from your previous location that's no longer relevant, you don't need to do anything formally. The connection is yours to manage directly with that person.

Changed workplace, not home?

The same approach applies — submit a new journey with the correct workplace, using the same email address.

💡
Try a wider distance too — If you're in a new area with fewer registered commuters, consider submitting with a slightly wider matching distance to increase your chances of finding a match sooner.
