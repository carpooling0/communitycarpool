⚙️ Managing Your Journey

Pausing or deactivating a journey

You can archive (deactivate) a journey directly from your matches page, or let it expire automatically. This page explains all your options.

Archiving a journey from your matches page

The easiest way to stop being matched for a journey is to archive it directly from your matches page. Open your matches page via your notification email, find the journey you want to stop, and click the Archive Journey button at the top of that journey's section.

Archiving is immediate — the journey is removed from match searches straight away. You can reactivate it at any time using the Reactivate Journey button that replaces it.

💡
Archiving keeps your matches — When you archive a journey, your existing match history and any mutual contacts are preserved. Only new matching is stopped.

How journeys deactivate automatically

All journeys have a built-in expiry mechanism. If no new matches are found within 30 days of your last match notification, your journey is automatically set to expired. You will receive an email letting you know.

Once expired, you will no longer appear in match searches. You can resubmit at any time to start fresh.

ℹ️
Expiry does not delete your account — Your name and email remain on file. Only the journey submission is deactivated. See Deleting your account if you want your data removed entirely.

Stopping match notifications early

If you want to stop receiving match notification emails before your journey expires naturally, you can unsubscribe from match emails using the link at the bottom of any notification email. This stops further email alerts without removing your journey from the system.

To manage all your email preferences in one place, see Managing email notifications.

Can't access your matches page?

If you no longer have access to your matches page link and need a journey deactivated, you can submit a request through the support page. Select Other / General Enquiry and include your registered email address and a brief description. Our team will deactivate the journey and confirm by email, usually within one working day.

Deleting your account entirely

If you want all of your data — including all journeys and your user record — permanently removed, use the account deletion option. See Deleting your account for full details.

💡
Circumstances changed? — If you've moved or changed workplace but want to stay in the pool, it's better to submit a new journey with your updated details rather than deactivating the old one. Both can run in parallel.
