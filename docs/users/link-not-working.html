🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

My matches link isn't working

The link in your match notification email opens a secure, personalised matches page. If the link isn't working, here are the most common causes and how to fix them.

The link has expired

Match access links are valid for 120 days from when they were issued. If you're trying to open a very old email, the link may have expired and will show an error page.

Fix: Find a more recent match notification email. Every time a new match is found for your journey, a fresh link is included in that email. Older links from previous emails also remain valid as long as they are within the 120-day window.

The link was broken by your email client

Some email clients (particularly mobile apps and certain webmail providers) wrap or truncate long URLs, cutting off part of the link. This makes the link invalid even if it looks complete.

Fix: Try one of the following:

  • Open the email on a different device or email client and try the link there.
  • Right-click the link and copy the URL, then paste it directly into your browser address bar. Check that the full URL is present and not truncated.
  • If the email shows the link as plain text (not a hyperlink), select the entire URL carefully — it may wrap across multiple lines.

Your account has been deleted

If you requested account deletion and the deletion has since been processed, your matches page will no longer be accessible. All associated data has been permanently removed.

A technical error on our end

Occasionally, a link may fail due to a temporary system issue. If you've checked the above and the link still doesn't work, contact us via the support page. Include the email address you registered with and we'll issue a fresh access link.

ℹ️
Your matches are not lost — The link is simply a secure access method. Your journey and any matches found are still in the system. A support request will get you back in.
