🛠️ Troubleshooting & Support

I expressed interest — what now?

Clicking Interested on a match does not immediately trigger any notification to the other person. What happens next depends on whether they also click Interested on your profile.

What clicking Interested actually does

When you click Interested, your decision is recorded privately. The other person is not notified that you expressed interest in them. Nothing visible changes on their matches page.

The system now waits for the other person to open their own matches page and independently click Interested on your profile. This is intentional — it ensures that contact details are only exchanged when both parties have chosen to connect.

If the other person hasn't responded

There are a few reasons why nothing may have happened yet:

  • They haven't checked their matches page yet — They may not have opened the notification email, or the email may have gone to spam. There is no way to know on your end.
  • They are considering it — The match appeared on their page but they haven't made a decision.
  • They clicked Not for me — If they declined, this is recorded but you are not notified. The match will remain visible on your page with a Pending status, but a mutual match will not be confirmed.
ℹ️
There is no timeout — A one-way interest decision doesn't expire. If the other person eventually opens their matches page and clicks Interested, a mutual match will be confirmed regardless of how much time has passed — as long as both journeys are still active.

When it becomes mutual

If the other person also clicks Interested, both of you receive an email notification immediately. When you open your matches page, the match card will show a green "You're both interested!" banner with the other person's full name and email address.

See What happens when it's mutual for full details on what to do next.

Can I change my decision?

Interest decisions cannot currently be reversed through the matches page. If you expressed interest by mistake and want to retract it before a mutual match is confirmed, contact us via the support page and we can help.

💡
Keep your journey active — You may have more matches coming. While waiting on one person to respond, the system continues to find and notify you of other compatible commuters.
