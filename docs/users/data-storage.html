🔒 Privacy & Your Data

What data we store & for how long

This page explains exactly what information Community Carpool collects, why it's needed, and how long it is retained.

Data we collect

DataWhy we collect itRetention
First namePersonalise emails and display masked version to matchesUntil account deletion
Email addressSend match notifications and secure access linksUntil account deletion
Home area (From location)Run the matching algorithmUntil account deletion
Workplace (To location)Run the matching algorithmUntil account deletion
Matching distance preferenceFilter matches to your toleranceUntil account deletion
Match decisions (Interested / Not for me)Determine mutual matches and prevent repeat matchingUntil account deletion
Email open/click eventsUnderstand notification delivery (anonymised)90 days
Support ticket contentHandle and resolve your request1 year after resolution

What we do NOT collect

  • Last name or surname
  • Phone number
  • Exact home address or apartment/villa number
  • Payment information (the service is free)
  • Precise GPS location or device location data
  • Browsing history or device fingerprint

Journey data retention

Active journeys are retained for 30 days following your last match notification. After this period, if no new matches have been found, your journey is automatically expired. You can resubmit at any time.

⚠️
Journey expiry ≠ account deletion — When a journey expires, only that journey's submission is deactivated. Your user record (name and email) is retained until you explicitly request account deletion. See Deleting your account.

Requesting your data

You can request a copy of all data we hold about you at any time by submitting a request via the support page. We will respond within 5 working days.
