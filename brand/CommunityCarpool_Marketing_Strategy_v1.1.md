## Role: "Campus Growth Intern"

### Responsibilities
1. **Community Management** (5 hours/week)
   - Monitor Reddit/Slack for commute-related posts
   - Share communitycarpool.org link (with your referral code)
   - Respond to DMs within 2 hours

2. **Campus Activation** (3 hours/week)
   - Post in campus groups (Slack, WhatsApp, Discord)
   - Share your referral link with 3-5 friends
   - Attend 1 campus event/month to promote

3. **Data Tracking** (2 hours/week)
   - Track signups from your referral link (built-in dashboard)
   - Submit weekly report (# signups, # matches, feedback)
   - Help identify new communities/groups to join

Simple page on communitycarpool.org/interns showing:
- Your unique referral link
- Your current signup count
- Leaderboard (top 5 interns)
- Resources (posting templates, guides)
